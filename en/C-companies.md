# Appendix C · Global Companies and Pipeline Map

> Who is doing what. Status may change; before publishing anything, check the latest company announcements.

---

## Top-Tier Big Pharma

**GSK (GlaxoSmithKline)**
- Lead: Bepirovirsen (ASO)
- Stage: Phase III B-Well
- Highlight: 2026 data showing ~20% functional cure rate

**Gilead Sciences**
- Lead: Bulevirtide, Selgantolimod (TLR8), multiple early-stage siRNA programs
- Highlight: A veteran player in the HBV space

**Roche / Genentech**
- Lead: Peg-IFN, multiple combination regimens
- Collaboration: With Dicerna (now Alnylam) on RG6346 (siRNA)

**Janssen (Johnson & Johnson)**
- Lead: Previously pushed the JNJ-3989 + Bersacapavir combination
- Status: Partial divestment/termination of HBV programs in 2022

**Novartis / Bristol Myers Squibb**
- Mainly peripheral involvement through investments and partnerships

---

## HBV Specialty Companies

**Vir Biotechnology (San Francisco)**
- Lead: VIR-2218 (siRNA) + VIR-3434 (antibody)
- Stage: Phase II/III
- Highlight: Dual-target combination approach

**Arrowhead Pharmaceuticals (California)**
- Lead: JNJ-3989 / ARO-HBV series (partnered with Janssen)
- Stage: Phase II/III
- Highlight: Mature siRNA platform

**Assembly Biosciences**
- Lead: ABI-H3733 (Capsid Modulator)
- Stage: Phase II
- Highlight: Class I CAM

**Aligos Therapeutics**
- Lead: Multiple Capsid and siRNA candidates
- Stage: Early clinical
- Highlight: Multi-platform

**Barinthus Bio (formerly Vaccitech)**
- Lead: VTP-300 (therapeutic vaccine)
- Stage: Phase II
- Highlight: Viral vector vaccine

**Precision BioSciences**
- Lead: ARCUS gene editing targeting cccDNA
- Stage: Preclinical → early Phase I
- Highlight: A CRISPR-space pioneer

**Excision BioTherapeutics**
- Lead: CRISPR antiviral
- Highlight: Prior experience with HIV CRISPR
