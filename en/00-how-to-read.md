## Conventions

I use a few consistent symbols throughout:

- **🧠 Note** — something worth pausing to think about
- **🔑 Drug target hint** — which drug class targets this step
- **📍 Key Points** — condensed summary at chapter end
- **⚠️ Data pending verification** — number needs to be checked against latest reports
- **✅ / ❌** — supported / not supported, effective / not effective

## The Most Important Sentence

> **This book cannot replace your doctor.**

Any decision about starting, stopping, or changing your medication belongs to you and your attending physician.

What this book can do is help you understand — during conversations with your doctor — what they're saying and why.

That's already much more than most patients have.
