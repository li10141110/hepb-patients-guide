# Appendix B · Drug Development Timeline

> From 1963 to 2040+, a chronology of HBV drug evolution.

---

## 1960s · Discovering the Virus

- **1963** — Baruch Blumberg discovers the "Australia antigen"
- **1965** — Its link to hepatitis is confirmed
- **1967** — The nature of HBsAg is established
- **1970** — Dane sees the complete virus under an electron microscope
- **1976** — Blumberg wins the Nobel Prize

## 1970s · Dawn of the Vaccine

- **1971** — First attempts at a serum-derived vaccine
- **1979** — Full sequencing of the HBV genome

## 1980s · Vaccine Era

- **1981** — First-generation plasma-derived vaccine (Heptavax-B)
- **1986** — Recombinant yeast vaccines launched (Recombivax HB, Engerix-B)
- **1980s** — Interferon α begins to be used for chronic hepatitis B

## 1990s · First-Generation Antivirals

- **1992** — China adds the HBV vaccine to its childhood immunization program
- **1998** — **Lamivudine** is approved, the first oral HBV drug
- **1998** — First evidence that nucleoside analogs achieve long-term viral suppression

## 2000s · Optimizing NUCs

- **2002** — **Adefovir** approved
- **2005** — **Entecavir (ETV)** approved
- **2006** — **Telbivudine** approved
- **2005** — Newborn vaccination becomes fully free in China
- **2008** — **Tenofovir Disoproxil Fumarate (TDF)** approved for HBV
- **2005** — First Peg-IFN approved for HBV

## 2010s · The TAF Era + Understanding cccDNA

- **2010** — Deeper research into cccDNA molecular mechanisms
- **2012** — NTCP identified as the HBV entry receptor (Yan et al.)
- **2014** — HCV direct-acting antiviral (DAA) revolution offers ideas for HBV
- **2015** — CRISPR first used to cleave cccDNA (preclinical)
- **2016** — **TAF (Vemlidy)** approved
- **2017** — Heplisav-B (CpG-adjuvanted vaccine) approved in the US
- **2018** — First siRNAs (ARO-HBV/JNJ-3989) enter clinical trials

## 2020-2025 · Eve of the New Drug Revolution

- **2020** — **Bulevirtide** approved in the EU (indication: HDV)
- **2020** — Bepirovirsen (ASO) Phase II data shows deep HBsAg decline
- **2020** — WHO 2030 elimination plan mid-term evaluation begins
- **2021** — AlphaFold accelerates protein structure prediction
- **2022** — Bersacapavir (Capsid) Phase III terminated — a CAM lesson
- **2022** — VTP-300 + PD-1 shows HBsAg seroconversion signals
- **2023** — VIR-2218 + VIR-3434 Phase II
- **2024** — WHO 2030 mid-term report: new infections on track, deaths not on track
- **2025** — Hopkins study: HBsAg source shifts from cccDNA to integrated DNA
- **2026** — **Bepirovirsen Phase III readout: about 20% functional cure rate** (GSK)

## 2027-2035 · The Cure Era

- **2027-2028** — Bepirovirsen expected approval
- **2028-2030** — siRNA + immune combination regimens expected to be approved
- **2030+** — Therapeutic vaccine combination regimens approved
- **2030+** — CRISPR early Phase I/II
- **2032+** — Individualized combination regimens become widespread
- **2035+** — Functional cure rate breaks 50%

## 2035-2050+ · Sterilizing Cure?

- **2035+** — CRISPR approved for special indications
- **2040+** — Sterilizing cure regimens enter routine use
- **2045+** — WHO elimination target largely achieved
- **2050+** — HBV drops from a major threat to background noise

---

## Some Key "Dead-Fire" Lessons

**Bersacapavir (Janssen)**
- Phase III terminated in 2022
- Lesson: CAM alone is not enough for a cure

**GS-4774 (Gilead therapeutic vaccine)**
- Phase II terminated
- Lesson: A protein vaccine alone is not potent enough

**Inarigivir (RIG-I agonist)**
- Terminated due to serious adverse events
- Lesson: Immune activation must be precise

**Every failure moves the field forward.**
