## Bepirovirsen (ASO)

**B-Clear (Phase II, completed)**
- ClinicalTrials.gov: NCT04449029
- Results: Yuen et al. NEJM 2022

**B-Well (Phase III, ongoing/readout)**
- ClinicalTrials.gov: NCT05630807
- Primary endpoint: Sustained HBsAg + DNA negativity at 24 weeks off-treatment
- **2026 data show about 20% meeting the primary endpoint**

**B-Fine (other Phase III)**
- Related studies, check ClinicalTrials.gov

## How to Search for the Latest Trials

**Step 1**: Open https://clinicaltrials.gov

**Step 2**: Search by:
- Drug name (Bepirovirsen)
- Disease (Hepatitis B)
- Phase (Phase 2/3)
- Status (Recruiting / Active / Completed)

**Step 3**: For each trial, you can find:
- Primary and secondary endpoints
- Inclusion and exclusion criteria
- Recruitment sites
- Target enrollment
- Estimated completion date
- Published results

**Step 4**: If interested:
- Contact the trial center
- Or ask your doctor

## A Piece of Advice

**If you want to join a trial:**

1. First find a target on ClinicalTrials.gov / chictr.org.cn
2. Contact your primary physician to have them assess your eligibility
3. Contact the trial center to ask about the enrollment process
4. Read the informed consent form carefully
5. Ask about: side effects, compensation, exit mechanism, follow-up requirements
6. **Never pay anyone to participate in a legitimate trial**
